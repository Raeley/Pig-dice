Pig dice

Fun family dice game!!

Rules of the game and how to play

  • Have a dice with all four sides labeled one to six.
  • First player rolls the dice
  • If roll is one,the player has lost the game and scores zero.If the roll is any other number,continue playing untill player decides to hold.
  • Second player rolls when it's his time and the cycle continues.
  • The tallies keep adding and the player who becomes first to score 100 becomes the winner
